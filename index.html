About the Course

This 8-week course, "Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity," focuses on leveraging no-code AI tools for task automation. It provides hands-on experience with Large Language Models (LLMs) to generate text, create websites, analyze data, and automate workflows, enhancing productivity in professional and research environments.

Module 1: Websites, Docs & Presentations

Learn how LLMs work, generate structured text (Markdown, HTML), build simple websites, and design presentations using AI assistants.

Module 2: AI-Automated Data Analysis

Master AI tools for data analysis, file manipulation, and creating automated reports and visualizations from datasets.

Module 3: GenAI for Research & Text Analysis

Explore AI techniques for literature searches, text analysis, knowledge synthesis, and extracting insights from large text collections.

Module 4: Custom GPTs and Chatbots

Build custom chatbots for research assistance and knowledge management, understanding LLM architecture and retrieval augmentation.

Module 5: AI for Task Automation

Discover strategies for automating repetitive research tasks, setting up email automations, and designing AI agents for complex workflows.

Module 6 & 7: Communication & Capstone

Learn to use AI for research dissemination by creating summaries and visuals, and complete a capstone project to showcase your skills.
